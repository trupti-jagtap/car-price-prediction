WHY DO WE NEED A CAR PRICE PREDICTION SYSTEM?

Every year, varied types of cars are manufactured. The prices of this cars are decided by the manufacturers taking into consideration various factors including the taxes. Getting a whole new car can cost much and rather feel as expensive to some customers. However, the dream to own a car even when the customers are in capable of spending the required funds have kept the market of the used cars ongoing. The used cars sales are on the global increase and that's why there is a need of a car price prediction system. A used car can't be bought or sold with the current market price of the same car model. But it is very essential that features like the current market price, the price of the car at the time when the car was bought, the kilometers it has covered etc to be taken into consideration to determine the price of the car for both buying and selling of the car.

This car price prediction system takes input such as the model of the car, the distance covered, the number of owners, the vehicle type (petrol/diesel) etc. from the users are predict the appropriate price that could serve as the buying/selling price.



HOW WOULD THIS PREDICTION HELP:

A) BUYER: Even though there are numerous sites that give online car price prediction, it is always better to double check the results. The buyer can analyze and cross check his price and compare it to that of the seller so that he can negotiate and can understand the pricing himself.

B)SELLER:This site would not only help the buyer but also the seller as well. Sellers can input the values as per asked and get a price for a certain model of a car. He can decide the price of the car of a specific model he sells with this information.



HOW ACCURATE IS THE CAR PRICE PREDICTION SYSTEM?

We have tested various Machine Learning models and came up with the best among them. The current model we are using for this website gives a 92% accuracy which is actually far more better than most of the others. This system gives a prediction based on the data from the past and hence the expected results might vary slightly than the predicted once.



NOTE:

The sole purpose of this website is predicting an appropriate price based on the input. It does not guarantee a perfect price as it does not take into consideration any default such as a dent, some problems with the parts etc. The value or the price may decrease due to the above said problems. Please make sure of all this points while deciding the price for your car.
